Overview

The Taj Mahal is widely admired for its serene presence and breathtaking beauty that draws millions of visitors each year. Set along the Yamuna River, it stands as a symbol of love and artistic excellence. Its setting, symmetry, and reflection in the surrounding gardens make it one of the most photographed landmarks in the world.

Visitors are often struck by the tranquil atmosphere that surrounds the monument. Its changing hues at different times of day create a memorable visual experience.
